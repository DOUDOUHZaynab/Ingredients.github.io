Ingredients

  • 400 g of flour
  • 100 g of soft butter
  • 15 cl of milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 150 g of pearl sugar
  • 25 g of fresh yeast
  • 1 packet of vanilla sugar
  • 1 pinch of salt

Preparation

  1. Warm the milk slightly and dissolve the yeast in it.
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, eggs, vanilla sugar, salt, and milk-yeast mixture. Knead well and let the dough rise for 30 minutes under a clean cloth.
  3. Add the soft butter, then mix in the pearl sugar. Shape the dough into 12 small balls and let them rest for 15 more minutes.
  4. Cook each dough ball in a waffle iron for 1-2 minutes.
